About Omni

Over two years of work by top minds has produced a fully secure decentralized exchange. Create, customize, trade and transact your own digital property, using Bitcoin as the rails.

Unpacking an Omnicore RPC server in a few lines is the easiest way to plug-in Bitcoin blockchain transactions with dApps in a multi-chain smart contract architecture.

Contributors to Omni have included thought leaders from: Mastercoin, Ethereum, Colored Coins, CounterParty, Factom, Tether, MaidSafe, Hive Wallet, HolyTransaction, Ribbit.me, Serica, Bitfinex and the Decentralized Apps Fund.

Omni Core desktop looks and feels like Bitcoin QT, but with more power, and Omnicored has been integrated with leading blockchain companies such as: Bitfinex, Poloniex, Fuzo's BitSIM, GoCoin, Expresscoin, ZenBox, Tether, HolyTransaction, Ambisafe, CryptoNext, Poloniex, C-CEX, Bittrex and BTER.

Assets on Omni Layer include: MaidSafe, Factoids redeemable on the Factom chain, LaZooz's ride sharing currency, Synereo's AMP currency for advertising on a decentralzied social network, Tether dollars integrated with Bitfinex, multiple issuers of vaulted-gold tokens, Impact Bonds for blockchain NGOs, and high-yield dollars (or any world currency) backed by hedged OMNI reserves. Native support for dynamic issuance/redemption, crowdsales and dividends. Decentralized futures contracts coming soon.

The Omni Foundation currently works with several international organizations that promote crypto-finance solutions for end users in Sierra Leone, Ghana, Botswana and Argentina.

Each day people learn what Omni can do and begin to leverage its potential.
